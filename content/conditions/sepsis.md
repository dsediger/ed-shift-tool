### Rapid screen
- Suspected infection
- qSOFA / SIRS criteria
- Lactate
- Blood cultures before antibiotics if possible

### Initial management
- Broad-spectrum antibiotics within 60 minutes
- 30 mL/kg crystalloid for hypotension or lactate >= 4 mmol/L
- Norepinephrine first-line vasopressor
