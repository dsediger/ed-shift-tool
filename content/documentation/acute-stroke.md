### Quick evaluation
- Last known well
- NIHSS assessment
- Blood glucose
- CT head without contrast immediately

### Red flags
- Sudden focal weakness
- Aphasia or dysarthria
- Vision changes
- Altered level of consciousness

### Management notes
- Activate stroke team
- Thrombolysis eligibility
- Consider mechanical thrombectomy if large vessel occlusion
